FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions from healthcare professionals about our services.

Is digital marketing for doctors allowed in India? ▼
Yes, digital marketing for healthcare is permitted in India within guidelines set by the National Medical Commission (NMC). Content must be informational, must not make false claims or guarantees, and cannot use prohibited testimonials in certain contexts. Practygo is fully trained in these regulations and ensures all your content and campaigns are compliant.
How long before I see results from SEO? ▼
Local SEO for healthcare typically shows meaningful results in 2–4 months. Google Business Profile improvements can yield faster results — often within 4–6 weeks. SEO is a long-term investment, but our local healthcare-focused strategies are designed to deliver early wins alongside sustained growth.
What is GEO and do I really need it? ▼
Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO) is about ensuring your practice appears when AI tools like ChatGPT, Google's AI Overviews, and Perplexity generate answers to patient queries. An estimated 30–40% of health-related queries are now going to AI tools rather than traditional search. While not mandatory today, GEO is quickly becoming essential — getting ahead now is a significant competitive advantage.
Do you work with all medical specialties? ▼
Yes! We work with doctors across all specialties — cardiologists, dermatologists, orthopaedic surgeons, gynaecologists, paediatricians, dentists, ophthalmologists, psychiatrists, and more. We also work with diagnostic centres, hospitals, wellness clinics, and Ayurvedic practitioners. Each strategy is fully customised for your specialty.
Are there any long-term contracts? ▼
No lock-in contracts. All our plans are month-to-month. We believe in earning your trust every month through results and transparency. That said, digital marketing builds momentum over time, so we always recommend at least a 3-month engagement to see meaningful, measurable outcomes.
Can you help with Practo and other health platforms? ▼
Absolutely. We manage and optimise profiles on Practo, JustDial, Sulekha, 1mg, and other Indian health directories as part of our SEO and Reputation Management services. A consistent, optimised presence across these platforms significantly boosts your local discoverability.
