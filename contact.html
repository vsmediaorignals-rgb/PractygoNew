Questions

Before You Reach Out

Quick answers to common questions from healthcare professionals.

Is the audit really free? What's the catch? ▼
Yes, completely free. We analyse your Google presence, website, and social media and share a personalised report with key recommendations. There's no obligation to work with us — we do it because we genuinely believe in helping healthcare professionals, and because great results speak for themselves.
Do you work with doctors outside the major metros? ▼
Absolutely. We serve healthcare professionals across India — from Mumbai and Delhi to smaller cities like Nagpur, Indore, Coimbatore, and beyond. In fact, tier-2 and tier-3 cities often present even more SEO opportunity since local competition is lower.
How involved do I need to be? ▼
We handle almost everything. You'll need around 1–2 hours per month for content approvals, brief feedback calls, and strategy check-ins. We know you're busy with patients — our entire workflow is designed to minimise your time investment while maximising your results.
Do you support Hindi content? ▼
Yes! We create bilingual content in both English and Hindi (and other regional languages on request). This is particularly effective for reaching patients in non-metro regions and for social media targeting across India's diverse linguistic landscape.
